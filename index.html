WHAT IS Trëzôr®?

Trëzôr® is the world's first and most secure cryptocurrency hardware wallet, designed to give you complete control over your digital assets while keeping them safe from online threats. Developed by SatoshiLabs, Trëzôr® provides an unmatched level of security for Bitcoin, Ethereum, and over 1,000 other cryptocurrencies.

Unlike software wallets that are vulnerable to hacking, Trëzôr® stores your private keys offline in a secure hardware device. This means your cryptocurrencies are protected even when using compromised computers. Trëzôr®'s open-source software allows for complete transparency, and its intuitive interface makes it accessible for both beginners and advanced users.

The name "Trëzôr®" comes from the Czech word for "vault," reflecting its purpose as a secure storage solution for your digital wealth. Since its launch in 2014, Trëzôr® has set the standard for hardware wallet security and continues to innovate in the cryptocurrency space.

UNMATCHED SECURITY FEATURES

Trëzôr®'s security architecture is built on multiple layers of protection to ensure your assets remain safe under all circumstances. The device generates and stores private keys completely offline, never exposing them to potentially compromised computers or networks. Each transaction must be physically confirmed on the device, preventing remote attacks.

The wallet features a secure element chip that's resistant to physical tampering and side-channel attacks. All firmware is open-source, allowing the community to audit and verify its security. Trëzôr® also implements a recovery seed system - a 12 to 24-word mnemonic phrase that can restore your wallet if the device is lost or damaged.

Additional security features include passphrase protection (creating hidden wallets), PIN code with increasing wait times after failed attempts, and the ability to wipe the device clean after too many incorrect PIN entries. Trëzôr®'s security model has been battle-tested for nearly a decade with no successful breaches reported.
